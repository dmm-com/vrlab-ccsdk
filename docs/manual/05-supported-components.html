Third-party Assets

Bakery

Lightmaps baked by Bakery can be used in your worlds. To do so:

  1. Copy the BakerySupport.cs file in the Extras~ folder into your project's Editor folder.
  2. Bake your lightmaps and probes with Bakery (we do not support SH and RMP lightmaps).
  3. Select Connect Chat > Extras > Copy Bakery Lightmaps from the menu. This will create a new object in your scene and initialize it using Bakery's lightmaps settings.
  4. Disable all Bakery related gameobjects.
  5. Build and upload your world.
